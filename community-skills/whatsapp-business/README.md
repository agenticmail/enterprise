# WhatsApp Business

Integrate with WhatsApp Business to send, send, get and more.

## Tools

- **Send Message** (`whatsapp_send_message`) — Send a text message via WhatsApp Business API. Requires the recipient phone number in international format and the message text.
- **Send Template** (`whatsapp_send_template`) — Send a pre-approved message template via WhatsApp Business API. Templates are required for initiating conversations.
- **Get Media** (`whatsapp_get_media`) — Retrieve media metadata (URL, MIME type, size) for a WhatsApp media ID. The URL can then be used to download the media.
- **List Templates** (`whatsapp_list_templates`) — List approved message templates for the WhatsApp Business account. Templates are required for initiating conversations with users.

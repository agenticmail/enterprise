# GoTo Meeting

Integrate with GoTo Meeting to list, create, get and more.

## Tools

- **List Meetings** (`goto_list_meetings`) — List upcoming and past GoTo Meetings. Filter by date range and whether to include historical meetings.
- **Create Meeting** (`goto_create_meeting`) — Create a new GoTo Meeting. Specify subject, start and end times, and optional password.
- **Get Meeting** (`goto_get_meeting`) — Get detailed information about a specific GoTo Meeting by its meeting ID.
- **Delete Meeting** (`goto_delete_meeting`) — Delete (cancel) a scheduled GoTo Meeting by its meeting ID. This action cannot be undone.

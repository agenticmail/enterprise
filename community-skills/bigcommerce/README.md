# BigCommerce

Integrate with BigCommerce to list, create, list and more.

## Tools

- **List Products** (`bc_list_products`) — List products in the BigCommerce store. Returns product names, prices, SKUs, and availability.
- **Create Product** (`bc_create_product`) — Create a new product in the BigCommerce store. Provide a name, price, type, and weight.
- **List Orders** (`bc_list_orders`) — List orders from the BigCommerce store. Optionally filter by status or date range.
- **List Customers** (`bc_list_customers`) — List customers in the BigCommerce store. Optionally filter by email or name.
- **Get Store Info** (`bc_get_store_info`) — Get general information about the BigCommerce store, including name, domain, plan, and status.

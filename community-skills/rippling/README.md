# Rippling HR

Integrate with Rippling HR to list, get, list and more.

## Tools

- **List Employees** (`rippling_list_employees`) — List employees from Rippling. Returns names, emails, titles, and department information.
- **Get Employee** (`rippling_get_employee`) — Get detailed information about a specific Rippling employee by their ID.
- **List Departments** (`rippling_list_departments`) — List all departments in Rippling. Returns department names, head counts, and leadership info.
- **List Teams** (`rippling_list_teams`) — List all teams in Rippling. Returns team names, members count, and team leads.
- **Get Company** (`rippling_get_company`) — Get company information from Rippling. Returns company name, address, employee count, and configuration.

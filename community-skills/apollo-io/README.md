# Apollo.io Sales Intelligence

Integrate with Apollo.io Sales Intelligence to search, enrich, search and more.

## Tools

- **Search People** (`apollo_search_people`) — Search for people in Apollo.io database. Filter by job title, company, location, and more.
- **Enrich Person** (`apollo_enrich_person`) — Enrich a person record in Apollo.io. Provide an email, LinkedIn URL, or name + company to get detailed information.
- **Search Organizations** (`apollo_search_organizations`) — Search for organizations (companies) in Apollo.io database. Filter by industry, size, location, and more.
- **Create Contact** (`apollo_create_contact`) — Create a new contact in your Apollo.io account. Provide at least an email address.
- **List Sequences** (`apollo_list_sequences`) — List email sequences in your Apollo.io account.

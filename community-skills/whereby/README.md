# Whereby

Integrate with Whereby to create, get, delete and more.

## Tools

- **Create Meeting** (`whereby_create_meeting`) — Create a new Whereby video meeting room. Specify an end date and optional room settings.
- **Get Meeting** (`whereby_get_meeting`) — Get details of a specific Whereby meeting room by its meeting ID.
- **Delete Meeting** (`whereby_delete_meeting`) — Delete a Whereby meeting room by its meeting ID. The room URL will no longer be accessible.
- **List Meetings** (`whereby_list_meetings`) — List all active Whereby meeting rooms. Returns room URLs, modes, and expiration dates.
